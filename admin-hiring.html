About Synergy

Great work starts with great people

Synergy Construction Group has been building across Chicagoland for over 16 years. As a General Contractor and Developer since 2009, our work spans multi-family housing, adaptive reuse, senior living, retail buildouts, and high-end interiors — big or small, commercial or residential.

We're proud of the reputation we've earned for being reliable, collaborative, and committed to delivering exceptional results. Our company is led by four hands-on owners who know the industry inside and out, backed by a team that genuinely enjoys working together.

With our main office in West Town, Chicago and a new satellite office in Lombard, we're continuing to grow — and we need a sharp, people-oriented administrator to help keep everything running.

The Role

What You'll Do

You'll be the face and voice of Synergy — supporting day-to-day office operations and the basic accounting tasks that keep everything running smoothly. No two days are exactly the same.

Answer and route incoming calls; greet visitors professionally
Manage deliveries and incoming/outgoing mail
Enter invoices and assist with credit card reconciliation
Support basic accounting tasks with accuracy and timeliness
Order and maintain office supplies
Assist subcontractors with waivers and documentation
Keep files organized — digital and physical
Maintain common areas: kitchen, conference rooms
Support office events and general coordination
Provide administrative support to leadership
Notarize documents as needed

Who We're Looking For

Qualifications

If you enjoy variety, staying organized, and being the go-to person in the office, we'd love to connect.

What You Bring

  • Experience in office administration, reception, or a similar role
  • Exposure to invoicing, reconciliation, or basic financial admin tasks
  • Strong organization and attention to detail
  • Clear, professional communication — written and verbal
  • Reliable, proactive, and team-oriented
  • Friendly, helpful attitude — you set the tone for the office

Nice to Have

  • Experience in a construction or real estate environment (preferred)
  • Familiarity with subcontractor documentation and lien waivers
  • Notary Public certification (or willingness to obtain)
  • Experience with accounting software or office management tools

Why This Role Matters

You're the front door to our company — and a key support to our internal operations. Your work directly impacts how we show up every day, both externally and internally.

You set the first impression

Clients, subcontractors, and partners meet Synergy through you. The tone you set matters.

Variety every day

From phones to invoices to events — no two days are identical. If you thrive on staying organized across different tasks, you'll fit right in.

A team that values you

We're a people-first company. Our four hands-on owners know every team member by name — and your contribution is visible and appreciated.
