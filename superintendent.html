About Synergy

Build With Purpose

Synergy Construction Group has spent 16+ years building a reputation for quality, collaboration, and integrity across Chicagoland. The Role

What You'll Do

You'll work closely with our Project Managers and General Superintendent to keep projects moving safely, efficiently, and at a high level of quality — from first shovel to final walkthrough.

Lead the Jobsite

  • Oversee daily field operations, inspections, and subcontractor coordination
  • Maintain project schedules through proactive planning and problem solving
  • Ensure materials and equipment are on-site when needed — no surprises

Build and Lead Strong Teams

  • Foster collaboration between owners, architects, inspectors, and trades
  • Run site walks, meetings, and check-ins that keep everyone aligned
  • Mentor field staff and hold teams accountable with a high level of respect

Own Safety and Quality

  • Enforce safety expectations in partnership with our Safety Coordinator
  • Address issues quickly and keep the jobsite compliant
  • Set and maintain high standards for workmanship

Communicate Proactively

  • Keep project stakeholders informed on progress, risks, and wins
  • Raise issues early to avoid delays
  • Recognize good work and keep teams motivated

Leverage Systems and Structure

  • Manage schedules, documentation, and reporting with consistency
  • Use technology to stay organized and keep projects running smoothly

Who We're Looking For

Qualifications

We care more about your track record on the jobsite than your resume format. Here's what matters.

Required

  • 7+ years in construction, including 5+ years as a Superintendent
  • Proven success leading ground-up, multi-unit, or multifamily projects
  • Strong leadership presence with subcontractors and field teams
  • Deep understanding of residential and commercial construction
  • Strong planning, scheduling, and communication skills
  • Proficiency in MS Office/Outlook
  • High school diploma and valid driver's license required

Nice to Have

  • Degree in Construction Management (preferred)
  • OSHA 30 certification (strongly preferred)
  • Experience with Sage, MasterBuilder, or SureTrak
  • Background across multi-family, senior living, and adaptive reuse projects
  • Existing relationships with Chicago-area trade partners
